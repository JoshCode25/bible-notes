translations:: [[Breath]], breeze

- from [[Greek/pneo]]; respiration, a breeze:
	- breath (1x)
	- wind {1x}
- Pnoe, akin to pneo, "to blow," lit., "a blowing," signifies
	- (1) "breath, the breath of life," Acts 17:25; and
	- (2) "wind," Acts 2:2.
	- Syn.: [[Greek/pneuma]] stresses strength, vigor, and force (Jn 3:8; Acts 2:2; 27:40). Pnoe by contrast is a gentle breeze, a quiet and calm exhalation.
