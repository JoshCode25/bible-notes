- (19x) deesis, *deh'-ay-sis*; from [[Greek/deomai]]; a petition:
	- prayer (12x)
	- supplication (6x)
	- request (1x)
- Deesis is prayer for particular benefits and [[Greek/proseuche]] is prayer in general.
