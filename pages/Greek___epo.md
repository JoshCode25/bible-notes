- a primary verb (used only in the def. past tense, the others being borrowed from [[Greek/ereo]], [[Greek/rheo]], and [[Greek/phemi]]); to speak or say (by word or writing):
	- say (859x)
	- speak (57x)
	- tell (41x)
	- command (8x)
	- bid (5x)
	- vr say (1x)
	- misc. (6x) = answer, bring word, call, grant.
